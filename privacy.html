PRIVACY POLICY

Privacy & Data Protection

How CITE CONTROL collects, uses, and protects your information.
Last updated: November 26, 2026

What We Collect

Account Information

We collect basic account details so you can sign in and use the app securely:

  • Email address or phone number (for login and recovery)
  • Profile details such as name, photo (optional), and safety preferences

Location Data

Location is the core of how CITE CONTROL works, but we keep it controlled:

  • GPS location when you mark a safe zone, request a Lookout, or use location-based safety features
  • Zone information for safe areas you create or interact with

Session & Interaction Data

  • Session metadata such as duration, pricing, and completion status
  • Chat messages exchanged during active Lookout sessions
  • Safety reports, incident details, and feedback you share

What We Never Do

Some things are completely off-limits:

  • We never sell your data to third parties.
  • We do not continuously track your location in the background.
  • We do not expose your exact GPS coordinates to other users or Lookouts.
  • We avoid storing unnecessary or unrelated data.

What Lookouts Can See

When you book a Lookout, they only see limited information needed to protect you effectively:

  • Your first name only / Not your full identity.
  • An approximate area around your position, not your precise GPS pin.
  • Chat messages during the active session only.
  • Session timing such as start and end times.

Lookouts do not see your exact coordinates, personal contact details, or your entire location history.

Data Deletion

Delete Your Account

You can request deletion at any time from Settings > Privacy > Delete Account.

  • Your account and related data are permanently deleted within 24 hours.
  • Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered.

What Gets Deleted

  • Profile information and preferences
  • Location data and safe zones
  • Session history and chat logs
  • Stored payment and subscription details (except records we must retain for legal or financial compliance)

Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for communication where applicable
  • Secure storage using industry standard practices
  • Strict internal access controls for authorized personnel only
  • Regular security reviews and best-practice updates

Your Controls

  • You can revoke location permissions at any time in your device settings.
  • You decide what profile information to share.
  • You can manage, export, or delete your data where supported.

Third-Party Services

CITE CONTROL integrates with trusted platforms to provide parts of the service:

  • Supabase for secure database and authentication
  • Stripe for payment processing (we do not store full card numbers locally)
  • Google Maps for mapping and location visualization
  • Expo as the application framework

Each provider has its own privacy practices, which apply when you interact with their services through our app.

International Users

Your data may be processed in different regions where our services and infrastructure operate, but we maintain consistent protection standards and aim to comply with local regulations where applicable.

Your Rights & Contact

You may have rights to:

  • Request access to the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for specific processing where applicable

If you have questions or requests about privacy, contact us:

  • Privacy: privacy@citecontrol.app
  • Support: support@citecontrol.app

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy as the product evolves. For significant changes, we’ll notify you and provide a clear effective date. Continued use of CITE CONTROL after changes go live means you accept the updated terms.
