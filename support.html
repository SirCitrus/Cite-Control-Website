Frequently Asked Questions

USER TYPES

Users are community members who report and verify incidents on the safety map. They earn XP for reports, verifications, and check-ins, and can book Lookouts for personal protection.

Lookouts are vetted safety professionals who monitor areas and respond to incident requests. They offer specialized services like bystander support, physical threat assessment, and intoxication monitoring. Lookouts earn income through bookings and gain recognition through the leaderboard system.

Agencies (security firms, municipalities) subscribe to Cite Control for premium features including custom app, advanced alert radius visibility, priority incident notices, historical heatmap data, and aggregated safety analytics for their jurisdictions.

Event Organizers use Cite Control to manage and inform large gatherings of available resources. They can access live incident maps, coordinate with professional Lookouts, and track crowd zones in real-time.

Apply through the Cite Control app to become a verified Lookout. To verify your professional credentials, you'll need to:

  • Upload your ID and professional verification documents
  • Upload a vulnerable sector check (background verification)
  • Verify your professional status: Police, Paramedic/Ambulance, Firefighter, Security Professional, Veteran, or other credentials

Once approved by our vetting team, specialization selected (based on your credentials and training), set your availability, and start accepting bookings to earn income.

MAP & SCREENS

MapScreen Used for reporting crisis and presence on the map. You'll see your alerts, Lookout availability, and SafeZones

CommunityScreen Shows all alerts, incident details, community discussions, and user discovery. Toggle between NOW mode (current incidents) and HISTORY mode (past incidents) to explore incident details, verify reports, and connect with other community members.

Use the MapScreen and Choose from 12 verifiable incident types:

Crisis Types (9 total): 6 prolonged crises (ongoing situations) and 3 immediate crises (urgent situations).

Presence Types (4 total): 4 presence types (Public service presence).

Lookout Requests (3 total): Request specific Lookout support for bystander help, physical threat response, or intoxication assistance.

The system uses smart duplicate detection—if a similar alert exists within 100m and 30 minutes, you'll be invited to verify instead of creating a duplicate.

Community verifications strengthen incident credibility. When an alert receives 3+ verifications, it's automatically marked as VERIFIED. Verified alerts remain visible on the map is extended for up to 60 minutes. You earn XP for both reporting and verifying incidents, plus bonus XP for verified reports.

Public Service Presence allows you to report the location of police, ambulance, firefighters, or security services on the map. This helps the community know what resources are available already or responding to an incident. These reports are visible for 30 minutes and can be verified by others to extend.

ZONES & SAFETY

Safe Zones are user defined areas you mark on the map as places where you feel secure. Create a custom Safe Zones around your home, workplace, or frequented locations. These appear on your personal map view to help you stay aware of your surroundings.

Crowd Zones are automatically shown as (SafeZones) and are generated by the system when high concentrations of people gather. They are auto created when 5+ community members gather in the same area OR when 3+ Lookouts gather. Crowd Zones help you identify where people are congregating and provide context for incident reports.

LOOKOUTS & BOOKING

Book a Lookout directly from the MapScreen or through the Community Hub. Select your desired specialization (bystander support, physical threat assessment, or intoxication monitoring) and choose your session duration: 15, 30, 45, or 60 minutes. Premium members have higher booking limits than free users.

Bystander Support: Lookouts help you stay safe in crowded or unfamiliar environments by providing overwatch, companionship or awareness.

Physical Threat Assessment: Specialized Lookouts trained to identify and respond to physical dangers and de escalate threatening situations.

Intoxication Monitoring: Lookouts compatible to assist if you've consumed substances, ensuring your safety and well being.

All Lookout bookings and payments are processed securely through Stripe integration. Your payment information is encrypted and stored securely. Only verified Lookouts can accept bookings, and all interactions are logged for safety and accountability.

GAMIFICATION & REWARDS

Earn XP by reporting incidents, verifying community reports, and participating in sessions. Your Level is calculated as: Level = floor(XP / 100) + 1. As your XP increases, your level grows, unlocking greater impact in the community and higher recognition on leaderboards.

Leaderboards rank community members and Lookouts by activity and XP. Badges recognize specific achievements like consistent reporting, high verification rates, and daily participation streaks. Both leaderboards and badges encourage community engagement and recognize top contributors.

Daily streaks track consecutive days you participate in Cite Control—reporting, verifying, or checking in. Maintain your streak to unlock bonus XP and recognition badges, encouraging regular community engagement.

PRIVACY & SECURITY

Your location settings and on map visibility are up to you, but ON while you're actively participating in a session or checking in. All location data is encrypted and protected by our Row Level Security policies. When you end a session, your location presence is removed from the map. See our Privacy Policy for complete details.

Your incident reports are visible on the public map to help the community respond to crises. Your identity as the reporter is visible to other community members. Reports include location, type, description, and verification count but no unnecessary personal details. Detailed verification documentation is stored securely and accessible only to relevant parties.

We collect your profile information, location data during sessions, incident reports, verifications, and Lookout booking history. We use this data to operate the platform, prevent fraud, improve safety features, and (for agencies and premium subscribers only) provide anonymized regional safety analytics. See our Privacy Policy for full details on data usage and retention.
